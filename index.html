About GW Jigs & Lures

GW Jigs & Lures is a small fishing lure company based in Texas.

I started making lures when I was 12 years old. I got into fishing with my baseball teammates out on Lake Conroe, and quickly developed a passion for it.

Bass fishing is my favorite, and every bait is made with that mindset.

This is a Christian-owned small business built on honesty, hard work, and appreciation for every customer.

Tight lines and God bless!
